SHANXI

As the Chinese 3A game Black Myth: Wukong became a hit, Shanxi went viral for its well preserved architectures of Buddhism, Taoism and Confucianism culture. During national holiday I traveled to it to look for these splendid temples, towers and memorial arches.

Date: 2024.9.30 ~ 2024.10.4

The clearly demarcated Jizhong Plain and Taihang Mountains.

The majestic Buddha and colourful frescoes are soothing and calming.

The calm atmosphere of Jin-style architecture.

Known as the above-ground museum, Shanxi has unique eaves and halls, truly eyecatching.

Ancient bronze statues glowed faintly in the darkness, as if telling of the glory of the past.

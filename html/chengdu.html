CHENGDU

The place where I now study. Located in the southeast part of China, it is a historic region with rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and, food! During my first year here, I have been to many places, and here are just some of the snapshots.

Date: 2023.8.24 ~ Present

The bridge where the 1986 The Dream of the Red Mansion TV series used as a filming site, definitely a shrine for me as a Hong enthusiast.

Chengdu was a surprise for me, as I didn't know much about it before, but who won't fall in love with a city with cute pandas and spicy hotpots?!

Chengdu is really a eco-city as parks can be find everywhere. I really enjoy taking a weekend afternoon citywalk in new discovered scenic spots.

Every city has its own mother river. Enjoy a peaceful night walking along the Jinjiang River with all those flashing LED, showcasing it's prosperity.

Unlike other metropolis in China, citizens of Chengdu has a sense of relaxation. Even the cat would prefer a snap disregard the surroundings.

Chengdu is not only home to the mysterious Samsung Mounds, it is also the sci-fi capital of China. Last year it held the 81th World Sci-fi Convention.
